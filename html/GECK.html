About GECK

Government Engineering College Kushalnagar (GECK), established in 2007, is a government-affiliated institution under Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi. Located in the serene backdrop of Kodagu, Karnataka, the college spans a 10-acre campus in the central part of Kushalnagar.

Courses Offered

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering

Infrastructure

The college provides a conducive academic atmosphere with well-equipped laboratories, spacious classrooms, and a library housing over 4,000 volumes. Additionally, there are more than 120 computers with internet connectivity at a speed of 10 Mbps.

Admissions

Admissions to GECK are primarily through the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET). The KCET 2025 registration is ongoing from January 23 to February 21, 2025, with the exam scheduled for April 16 and 17, 2025.

Placements

The placement statistics for the 2021-22 batch indicate promising outcomes:

  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE): 70% placement rate
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE): 60% placement rate
  • Mechanical Engineering: 56% placement rate
  • Civil Engineering: 5.2% placement rate
